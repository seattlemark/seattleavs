Welcome to Seattle AV Source
Seattle's premier supplier of A/V equipment rentals for projection, lighting, video, audio, staging and drapery equipment

Seattle Audio-Video Source is a cooperation of Data / Video Projection, Lighting, Sound and AV Labor Specialists in the Audio-Video Industry. Doug Kennelly went in business for himself April 15th, 1980 and throughout the years, Doug Kennelly has worked with many business owners and the idea of creating this co-operative was established to provide our clients with the best in Services, Equipment and discounted AV Rental Pricing.

Data Projection

Have data projection requirements? SeattleAVS has you covered. From short throw at the end of a conference table to scaffolding mounted projection for a convention with 500 people, we have the projectors, screens, cables, connectivity and knowledgable technicians to do the job right the first time.

Lighting

We are experienced at creating the right mood for your environment. Whether you need ambient back lighting for an auction or stage lighting for a concert, we have the design skills, equipment and manpower to expertly serve your needs.

Audio

We have a full line of custom professional pro-aodio reinforcement solutions from small public address systems to full size sound systems for auctions, concerts, conferences and conventions.

Staging / Drapery

We have staging, drapery and pipe (stands) to make your event look clean, polished and professional. Dress up your auction tables with lighting and drapery for an unforgettable look and feel.
